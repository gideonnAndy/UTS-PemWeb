Profile

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, born on February 5, 1985, is a professional football player from Portugal. He is currently recognized as one of the greatest players of all time. With speed, technical ability, and a knack for scoring goals, Ronaldo has won numerous prestigious titles with the Portugal national team and top European clubs.

Date of Birth: February 5, 1985

Origin: Funchal, Madeira, Portugal

Position: Forward

Main Achievements

  • 5x Ballon d'Or
  • 4x European Golden Shoes
  • UEFA Champions League Champion (5 times)
  • EURO 2016 Champion with Portugal
  • All-Time Top Scorer of Real Madrid
